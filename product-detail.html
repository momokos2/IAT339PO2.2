Decription

The Braden captures the cultural vibe of Northern California. This modern rectangle for men and women projects a fashion-forward, erudite look. The Sausalito is for anyone who wants to make a sophisticated and chic statement with their eyeglasses or sunglasses. We gave the classic square style a modern profile with more angular lines and narrow temple arms, as well as a deeper bridge.
